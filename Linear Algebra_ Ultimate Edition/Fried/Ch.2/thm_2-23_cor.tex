	Let $A \in M_{n \times n}(\mathrm{F})$ and $\gamma$ be an order basis of $\mathrm{F}^n \implies [L_A]_{\gamma} = Q^{-1}AQ$ where $Q$ is the $n \times n$ matrix whose $j$th column is the $j$th vector of $\gamma$
